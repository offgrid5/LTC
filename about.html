Why LTC Engineering

At LTC Engineering, we redefine the standard for infrastructure solutions by seamlessly integrating international expertise and industry best practices into every facet of our services. Here's why our approach is unique:

Access Infrastructure:

Our program management and engineering prowess go beyond conventional boundaries, providing a turnkey service for access infrastructure deployment. From innovative cabling topologies to site preparation, fire protection, civils, security, air conditioning, and comprehensive service management, LTC Engineering crafts an integrated solution that transcends industry norms.

Electrical Infrastructures:

In Botswana, LTC Engineering is at the forefront of engineering and implementing point-to-point and multi-point electrical networks.

Our ability to perform site and path surveys, coupled with advanced network capacity planning, positions us as the go-to choice for the turnkey implementation of wired networks.

Analysis of Technology:

Understanding your needs is our priority. We conduct technology demand studies, analyze user profiles, define network functional requirements, and carefully consider applicable technologies. This ensures that our solutions align precisely with your expectations and the latest industry advancements.

Definition of Concept Solution:

As an independent system engineering house, LTC Engineering prioritizes finding the optimum solution during the concept design phase.

Our focus is on creating a concept solution based on the most suitable technology and product, independent of manufacturer or technology platform, providing you with a validated reference for informed management decisions.
